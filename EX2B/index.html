History of REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) in Chennai was established in 1997 and has been a leader in engineering education. As an affiliate of Anna University Chennai and approved by AICTE, REC is known for providing quality education and promoting innovation. The college offers bachelor's and master's programs across different fields, giving students a well-rounded learning experience. REC also takes pride in its diverse certification offerings, which help students gain globally recognized credentials. REC offers a variety of affordable fees to cater to students from different financial backgrounds. The college is dedicated to the well-rounded growth of its students, as evident from its impressive job placement record. With an average salary package that is competitive with industry norms, REC ensures its graduates enter the professional world feeling confident and capable. The placement data demonstrates REC's unwavering commitment to providing its students ample opportunities to launch their careers and fulfill their aspirations.
